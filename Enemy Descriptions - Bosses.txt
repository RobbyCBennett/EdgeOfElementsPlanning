1: Dreeovni Woods
Inpenetrable Deceiver: Golem Nymph
She has a dress and hair of bewitched ivy. She has 2 medium thin blades. She will be quick and do spin attacks. Attack the vines and you will be ensnared. Once she is slow after a spin attack, smash her arms with the mace a few times to break off the stone shell. After breaking both arms, the cracks spread and reveal a pale nymph covered in ivy. Then she will summon bewitched ivy to try to kill you. Avoid the ivy and kill her.

You arrive in a quick room with a small window to see the full moon. You hear a loud, deep, monstrous howl and then the moon blocks out. Then it returns. You look behind you and see glowing green eyes in a shadow by a tunnel.
Beast of the Forest: Ironfang - A giant wolf with an iron bow, armor, fangs, and claws. Once you look into it's green eyes, bewitched ivy appears and grabs you, then it will bite and claw you, which will hurt but be less effective. If you blind it with guidance, when it stares at you, it will be blinded and weakened. It can also shoot poison arrows at you with its bow. It can also howl and summon lots of wolf archers to attack you. It's a nasty one.
