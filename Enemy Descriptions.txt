Skilled Fighter w/ Sword Breaker
	Wield a longsword in one hand and a sword breaker in the other.
	A sword breaker is a fancy name for a toothed dagger that catches blades.

Merfolk
	Beautiful and majestic creatures.
	They will sneak up to you, or lure you to them.
	Then, they will stab you with a harpoon and drag you down underwater with chains and drown you.

Squidbats
	Attack telegraph:
	While they're chilling on the ceiling, they will leak water down.
	If you pay attention, you'll notice the dripping.
	If you don't and you walk under them, they'll drop down immediately and land on your face, gouge out your eyes, and ruin your plans for the evening.
