Attacks:
	Shield bash
	Shield block
	Stab, left/right slash

Dungeon Design:
	Nonlinear locked doors. If you find 1 key, you can choose between 2 or more locked doors, increasing the sense of breadth of the dungeon.
	Hints: when hints are turned on, there will be a subtle sound from your guide when you enter a room with a key.

Collect weapons that you can find through hard side quests and also the main quest.

Weapons and Equipment:
	Edgewood:
	Woodland Axe (Required)
	Woodland Mace (Required)
	Woodland Brigandine (Required)
	Woodland Shield (Required)

Possible Powers:
	Red: Bleeding
	Orange: Fire (Damages enemies over time)
	Yellow: Lightning
	Green: Vines (Slowly makes a trap to ensnare enemies)
	Light Blue: Ice (Instantly ensnares enemies)
	Blue: Water
	Violet: Will-Bending
	White: Guidance (Weapon lights up as you are facing a goal)
	Black: Cloud of Darkness (Disables item use)
	Gray: Earth (Cool ranged attack while breaking ground)

Puzzles like from Portal, Antichamber, Zelda...

All weapons you collect can use your collected elemental skills, which are useful in different cases. You can briefly try out new powers
by defeating enemies that possess those elements. These elements also are helpful for puzzles.

Engaging side quests. Cool places to explore with crazy challenges, like "50 floors of doom"

Ability to decrease carrying capacity of items in exchange for carrying more ammo
