Writing
	Connections
		Have a connected history for each town, and for the people

	Environment Storytelling
		Active Environment Storytelling - Instead of hearing about how bad the empire is, experience it yourself

	Lore
		Explain phenomena. How do boats fly? How did the necromancer get his power? How did the winged sisters get their powers? How did they get their wings?

		Explain motivations and beliefs. What deities do people believe in? Why? Why are people part of the empire? Why do people fight back? Why do people do neither?

	Liars and Truthtellers
		It's a complicated world, and people interpret it differently. Have some characters tell lies about the opposing sides, which are driven by their strong convictions for their beliefs.

Level & World Design
	Make the worlds and levels interesting to play, even it was all gray.
