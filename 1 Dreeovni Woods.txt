Dreeovni Woods Chapter 0: Passage

You are little. You, your parents, and your little sister Myan are finishing packing the skyboat. Your father is wearing his usual work tunic as the King's guard. Some houses are on fire. You hear a warhorn. "We have to go now!" says your dad. Your sister sparatically drops her things and runs to the house. You run after her to get her to safety. You see her pick up a glowing blue crystal on a necklace from under her pillow. "Happy birthday, [Name]. I found this by a creepy cave. It lights up when everything else is dark, like you. Do you promise to take me there to find more?"

The warhorn is beating against your eardrums.

"NOW!!!" Your dad rushes in and grabs both of you. He rushes out the door, carrying you both. As he runs to the boat, he is struck by an arrow. He sets you both down "I love you! Don't look back. Be brave. Run to Mother. Go!" You both run into your mother's arms in the boat. He fights off many of the invaders, but he is struck down by more arrows and stabbed.

You all embark into the clouds, unfollowed.

Dreeovni Woods Chapter 1: Nightfall

Since it's almost harvesting season, you were trying to rest early tonight. You awake in the evening of your home of Dreeovni Woods to your little sister Myan shaking you. She says that she forgot the family's axe in the forest and doesn't want it to get rained on tonight, and the city gate has already closed. You wake up and can explore. There are not many sidequests, because life is good in Dreeovni Woods.

Dreeovni Woods Chapter 2: Isolation
(You awaken without your items in the dungeon of the castle of the capital Alavance. Knock out a guard with a rock and take his key. You find your armor/potions and necklace in the hands of guards fighting over it. Sneak through the dungeon without getting killed. Escape through stealth, not through the main gate.

You see a blue mystifying woman with a a gilded gown, swan-like mask, wings, and flowing sleeves. You also see your axe discarded nearby.

"My name is Nibeesa. What is yours?"

"(Name)"

"(Name), I see that you are a vailiant and courageous (lad/lass). See those soldiers there? These men do not see their evil actions, but their hearts are pure. If you wish to restore freedom to this land, follow me."

She flies to the cave and guides you through it.

Navigate through the Cave of Skulls, which is heavily protected by guards outside and monsters inside. There are blue glowing gems, just like your necklace. At the second to last room, you have to rush in the last doorway and the way closes. Then, a large stone carved into a skull has a small mystical skull-shaped gem inside of the eye socket. As you pick it up, a crack in the gem glows. Nibeesa appears in her mortal form, in color. She has a black and silver-gilded gown, swan-like mask, silver wings, and flowing sleeves.

She shows you some cave drawings that illustrate the story.

"This cave was formed that it might seal my power, stored in this gem, and that of my sister. While I wish for the destruction of the gems, there is a greater cause now. Many moons ago, an adventurer heard of the great powers in this cavern. She had lost her loved ones to the hand of war. She desired to stop the brutality, no matter the cost. My sister, Cleeshnia, tricked her. She took the adventurer's mortal frame and possessed it."

"She attempted to destroy this gem. I saw the fires of dark ambition in her eyes, and so I protected the stone that my powers might be used for good. Unable to extinguish my life, she fractured the gem and took my powers. I wish for both our powers to be sealed away."

(She takes off her mask to reveal her scar.)

"Will you help me regain my powers and put an end to her growing dominion of this world?"

(If you disagree at first):
"I see good in you. I understand if you feel overwhelmed. But, I will help you. You will not be alone."
(You finally agree).

(If you agree):
"The guards and monsters protecting this cave must be approaching soon. We must return the rest of my powers, which I sense Cleeshnia has scattered across the islands. I will direct you and always be by your side."

She goes back into the crystal, which goes into a ring on your hand. You have a new ability: guidance. Your sword glows white and shines light towards a new exit. Nibeesa will attempt to give you advice when discovering new areas, stuck in a certain area, and when fighting new enemies. You can accept or ignore her from materializing and talking to you. You can also turn off the feature entirely.

You roll a large skull-carved stone out of the way which is a shortcut at the back of the cave. Nibeesa guides you to run towards an abandoned skydock.
At this time you have arrived at the abandoned skydock. If you linger too long, you'll get captured by Cleeshnia herself and become a mindless soldier and lose. You see an old crappy sailboat and take it and fly home. The camera zooms out to show the boat fading into the sunset.

Dreeovni Woods Chapter 3: Home
As you draw near home, the town bears the insignia of Cleeshnia. Rosilila appears.
"Oh, [player], I'm so sorry." She hugs you. "I hope your sister and mother are okay. Let's make sure not another town has to go through this nightmare." You nod, and then she returns to the ring.

Some soldiers remain. Some villagers that fought back are now guards elsewhere. Those that didn't fight remain and must pay taxes to the Optika Empire. The villagers are in grief because of those that were taken by the dark magic.
