Powering Up
	1. Gaining more elements
	2. More weapons
	3. More protection
	4. Specific element study
	5. Weapon study
	6. Element combo study
	7. More health
	8. More mana for special attacks
	9. Item improvement
	10. Boat improvement

1.	(Main Quest) You gain more elements by beating bosses. You beat them with their own power, and you get it permanently.

2.	(Main Quest) You get each weapon in the main quest, usually in dungeons.

3.	(Main Quest) You get each armor/shield in the main quest, usually in dungeons.

4.	(Side Quest) You power up the elements by training from a shaman. He teaches you of the power of focusing all of your soul on one power. You can do a special devastating attack for one element.

5.	(Side Quest) You can study each weapon. By doing so, you are able to use combos with it.

6.	(Side Quest) You can study the combination of elements, for example fire and sand yields glass, and water and earth yields mud.

7.	(Side Quest) People will give you heart gems by showing them kindness.

8.	(Side Quest) Mana vitality is improved by practicing with some ancient master warlock.

9.	(Side Quest or Shop) You can improve certain items through magic or physical means. This could be a blacksmith working on your item because of a sidequest, a better arrow type unlocked, etc.

10.	(Shop) You can get more cannons, faster sails, better durability, etc.
