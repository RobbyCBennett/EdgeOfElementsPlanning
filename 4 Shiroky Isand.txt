(Confronting Cleeshnia)


Cleeshnia:
"You do not value life as I do. You are relentless. I am merciful. You are unforgiving. I am gracious. You are known by only one name in this land Nightmare. And rightly so. You feed on the blood and souls of the masses, just to have your way, to resume the calamity. As an accommodating host, I will give you the chaos that you seek."

Her eyes glow red. She turns around in her backless gown. You see a black and red hourglass tattoo on her back, an intricate recreation of a black widow symbol. She sprouts 6 more arms. Her skin turns ink black and the tattoo turns into a ruby.

Cleeshnia's Final Form:
	Bloodcrawler: Arachnid of Carnage
